About Us

We Listen First, Then We Treat

We know how exhausting persistent pain can be — the missed workouts, the broken sleep, the simple things that suddenly feel hard. At Niti Physiocare in Bengaluru, we start by truly understanding your pain and your goals, then build a treatment plan around them.

Whether it's nagging back pain, a stiff knee, a sports injury or recovery after surgery, our caring team blends evidence-based physiotherapy with hands-on, patient-first care — so you can get back to the life you love, faster.

What We Offer

Our Physiotherapy Services

Core rehabilitation programmes plus specialised therapies, all under one roof.

Orthopedic Physiotherapy

Treatment for back & neck pain, joint pain, arthritis, sports injuries, fractures and post-surgical recovery — restoring strength, flexibility and movement.

Geriatric Physiotherapy

Specialised care for older adults — improving balance, mobility and independence, managing arthritis, osteoporosis and reducing the risk of falls.

Sujok Therapy Add-on

A gentle, drug-free acupressure technique applied to the hands and feet to relieve pain and support the body's natural healing — offered as a complementary therapy.

Cupping Therapy Add-on

Traditional cupping to ease muscle tension, improve circulation and accelerate recovery — a popular complement to manual physiotherapy.
